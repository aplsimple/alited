 The non-standard settings of <r>Find/Replace</r>
 dialogue are

   <b>Find by List</b> checkbox at <b>Find</b> field:
     allows finding words of <b>Find</b> field
     (words are separated by spaces)

   <b>Paste "Find"</b> button at <b>Replace</b> field:
     copies <b>Find</b> field to <b>Replace</b> field

   <b>RE2</b> button: allows applying additional
     <b>regexp</b> conditions to the search result

   <b>Redraw</b> button: switches to/from standard
     geometry of <r>Find/Replace</r> dialogue

   <b>-geometry</b> checkbox: if set, allows saving
     current geometry of <r>Find/Replace</r>
     dialogue at closing it

   <b>Replace by blank</b> checkbox: allows blank
     replacements, just removing the found
     strings
