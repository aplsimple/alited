 Use buttons on the right to add/change/delete a list of favorites.

 The current list of favorites is displayed in the bottom listbox.
 Enter a name for the list and click "Add" button to use it aftewards.

 This way you can keep a kind of "projects inside a project", choosing
 a list of favorites for appropriate tasks inside a current project.

 For small tasks, click "kept" button to work with other open files.

 For big tasks, click "closed" button to work with the favorites only.

 Click "Back" button to return to an original list of favorites
 that were active initially, at starting the application.

 Click "Select" button or double-click a list to make it active.

 NOTE: If you consider the current favorites somehow significant,
 save them under some name, let it be even "tmp".
