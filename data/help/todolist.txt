 In this dialogue you can:

   - view all pending TODOs

   - filter TODOs: use <b>Filter</b> entry
     (the filter is case-insensitive)

   - modify any TODO: use <b>its text</b> field

   - erase a TODO at all: use <b>Erase</b> tool button

   - move TODO by day/week: use <b>Move</b> tool buttons

   - select a project to resolve its TODOs: just
     <b>double-click</b> its item or use <b>Select</b> button

 The TODOs are sorted by projects and dates, the
 overdue ones are highlighted with red & magenta.
