
 In the "Tcl files' extensions" field, you can set any
 additional extensions for the Tcl files you use.

 Enter the extensions separated by spaces.

 Also, you can set your own colors to highlight the
 appropriate constructs of Tcl.

 Use "Default" button to restore the default colors
 for highlighting.

 At need, you can enter your own commands to highlight,
 in the text field. They are highlighted as Tk ones.
