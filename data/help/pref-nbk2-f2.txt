 In the <b>Tcl files</b> extensions" field, you can set additional extensions
 for the Tcl files you use (for example, .bawt).

 Enter the extensions separated by spaces.

 Also, you can redefine colors for the appropriate constructs of Tcl.

 Use <b>Default</b> buttons to set one of the default color sets.

 At need, you can enter <b>your own commands</b> to highlight, in the text field.
 They are highlighted in the same color as Tk commands.

 In the <b>Code snippet</b> field, you can check these settings "in vitro".
